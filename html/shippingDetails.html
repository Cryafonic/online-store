Delivery Details

Irrespective of the delivery method you choose, we always take responsibility up to the point of delivery, i.e. if your parcel goes missing or the contents are damaged, we will replace the affected items at no cost to you.

Please note that the time taken to ship an item is listed on each product's detail page. For example, "Usually shipped within 2-5 working days" means that it will take us 2-5 working days to get the product from our supplier. The delivery times listed below apply once we ship the item to you.
We currently offer four methods of delivery.

Detail

Courier. These are door-to-door services. The delivery address must be a physical address, and cannot be a post office box. Someone will need to be available at the address to receive the delivery. Deliveries are made during business hours from Monday to Friday. We currently offer three courier options.

  • Priority courier. In main centres, this will reach you the next business day after we ship your order.
  • Standard courier. In main centres, this will reach you within 1- 2 business days after we ship your order.
  • Economy courier. In main centres, this will reach you within 1 - 3 business days after we ship your order.

Main centres include properties within a 50km radius of the major cities in South Africa. If you are not in a main centre, it may take a few days longer. We do not deliver by courier to plots, farms, mines, military bases, major chain stores, power stations, game reserves and lodges, airports or harbours, and should your delivery address fall into one of these categories you will be liable for the extra costs incurred in delivering or returning the parcel.

Pargo- Click and Collect. Powered by Pargo, The online store can now deliver to 2600 pick up points nationwide. Pargo offers the option to collect your parcels at the closest pick up point to you. Once your order has been shipped, your parcel should be ready for collection within 2-5 business days, however, Pargo will notify you via sms and/or email when it is at your chosen Pargo pick up point. Please note, currently we only offer this option for parcels that are under 7kgs. To track or for more information on pargo click here.

Overnight post office counter. This is a next business day service where the parcel will be ready for collection from your local post office counter one business day after we ship it. If you are not in a main centre, it may take 1-2 days. We offer delivery of parcels up to 2kg for R45, or for free if the order value is over R400. Delivery on orders over 2kg is calculated based on mass, value, and number of items in the order. To track shipping or for more information about your local Post Office click here.

Collection. You can also collect your parcel from our office at in the Westlake Business Park. For directions to our office Read more... Our office hours are Monday to Friday 8:30am - 5pm, and we're open for collections on Saturday from 8:30am to 1pm. We are not open on Sundays or public holidays. We don't charge for delivery if you choose to collect.

Free delivery options are subject to change.
